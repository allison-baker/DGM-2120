About Me

My name is Al Baker and I write all the content for this website! I've been doing photography for about 5 years now. I started photography with a hybrid camera that I picked up at an estate sale in 2016. I'm completely self taught, through lots of YouTube videos, practice, and collaboration with other photographers. I now run my own side photography business where I shoot couples photography, portraits, events, and more. I currently shoot with a Canon EOS Rebel T6, which is a beginner level DSLR that I'm hoping to upgrade soon. I'm very passionate about what I do and I love to share what I've learned with others, which is why I created this website. I hope you can learn something here!
