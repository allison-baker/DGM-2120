Home

Welcome to Budget Vacations! We're an enthusiastic group of world travelers who have made it our mission to curate the best travel deal packages for you so you can explore the globe for the best price. We have packages for all sorts of destinations on every continent, so wherever you're itching to go, we can help!

Our History

Budget Vacations began in 2019 in Provo, UT. The founders of Budget Vacations met at Brigham Young University and decided to use their shared love of budget traveling to build a company that curates travel for others. Since then, we've helped families and individuals find their perfect getaways for cheap - like only broke college students can do!

Our Clients

We cater to any travel lover, but our main clientele are people just like us - individuals and families who just don't have the biggest budget. College students especially like to hire us because used to be right where they are, traveling as full time college students with very little money!

Planning vacations can be stressful, we know that first hand. Don't let the stress of vacation planning overwhelm you and prevent you from having the fun and relaxing time you deserve! We handle it all, from the transportation to the lodging and the activities available near you. Contact us through the form on our Contact page for a free quote today!
