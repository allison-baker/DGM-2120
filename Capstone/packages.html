Packages

We have three travel packages curated with your specific needs in mind. Each package varies depending on the number of travelers. Each package baseline length of stay is one week, but we can adjust it to the length of your liking! If a package is close to what you need but not quite, feel free to message us on our Contact page and we'd love to work with you to customize one of the packages to suit your needs better!

Europe Package

Our Europe package includes round trips flights to a city of your choice through a European international airline, rental car, hotel rooms for the duration of your stay, and a curated itinerary with meals and activities.

Airline: Eurowings

Rental Car: Auto Europe

Hotel: Ibis Budget

Price: Starting at $2,000 per person
